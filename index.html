Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does it work?

    EZ Fitness is an extremely simple and easy way to get daily, tailored workouts to suit your needs. First, click on the Get Started button to select your muscle group. After that, you will see specific workouts that suit your needs.

  • Who should use it?

    EZ Fitness is mainly designed for college students who do not have much experience working out but would like to begin their fitness journey. However, anyone who desires a simple, effective workout would greatly benefit from EZ Fitness.

  • What if I have a dynamic schedule?

    No problem! EZ Fitness provides various training workouts for times ranging from 5 minutes to 30 minutes.

  • What if I don't have access to a gym?

    EZ Fitness understands that access to a gym may not be possible. So, we have provided workouts for those that do not have any gym equipment.

  • What if I don't know which muscle group to train first?

    Although training all muscle groups are important, we recommend that beginners focus on the abs/obliques and the biceps as those are the most used muscles in physical activities.
