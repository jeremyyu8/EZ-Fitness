About

EZ Fitness is designed to

  • Provide college students an easy way to choose a workout based on what they wish to exercise
  • Allow students to specialize their workouts and be more efficient with their time
  • Plan workouts over multiple days by selecting different muscle groups to train each day

We believe that exercise is essential in maintaining a healthy lifestyle, especially for college students who often live very stressful lives. So, we designed EZ Fitness, which provides an easy way for students to workout in either the comfort of their homes or in the gym. Additionally, we have specially designed workout sets for different durations in order to fit the busy lifestyles of college students.

Our website provides you with the best possible gym experience through

  • Simple and intuitive interface
  • Tailored workouts for specific needs
  • Targeted muscle groups for precise training
  • Comprehensive guidance on each exercise

Our goal is to provide great experiences for new college students looking to make a change to a healthier lifestyle. We, as students, understand how daunting going to the gym for the first time is. However, with EZ Fitness, anyone will have the ability to work on their body no matter where they are or how busy they are.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does it work?

    EZ Fitness is an extremely simple and easy way to get daily, tailored workouts to suit your needs. First, click on the Get Started button to select your muscle group. After that, you will see specific workouts that suit your needs.

  • Who should use it?

    EZ Fitness is mainly designed for college students who do not have much experience working out but would like to begin their fitness journey. However, anyone who desires a simple, effective workout would greatly benefit from EZ Fitness.

  • What if I have a dynamic schedule?

    No problem! EZ Fitness provides various training workouts for times ranging from 5 minutes to 30 minutes.

  • What if I don't have access to a gym?

    EZ Fitness understands that access to a gym may not be possible. So, we have provided workouts for those that do not have any gym equipment.

  • What if I don't know which muscle group to train first?

    Although training all muscle groups are important, we recommend that beginners focus on the abs/obliques and the biceps as those are the most used muscles in physical activities.
